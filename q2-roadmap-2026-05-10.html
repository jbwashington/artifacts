# Roadmap DSL v1

## Purpose
Capture roadmap items grouped by quarter, with status and metadata, in a flat append-friendly format.

## Syntax
Lines starting with `## ` open a quarter section: `## Q2`
Lines starting with `- ` are items: `- [status] title (k:v, k:v)`
Lines starting with `#` (not `##`) are comments — attached as a note to the most recent item.
Blank lines are ignored.

## Field values
status: now | next | later | done | blocked   (required, in brackets)
metadata keys (all optional, comma-separated inside parens):
  size: S | M | L | XL
  owner: <initials>
  risk: low | med | high
  due:  YYYY-MM-DD

## Editing tips
- To add an item, type one new bullet at the end of its quarter section.
- To re-status, change the bracketed value.
- To annotate, add a `# comment` line right after the item.
- Order within a section is preserved in render.
- Unknown keys are shown as raw `k:v` chips — they don't break parsing.

## Versioning
v1 (2026-05-10): initial; comments attach to preceding item as notes
